Sydney Opera House

The Sydney Opera house was created on the 2cnd of March 1959. The inventor of this marvellous design, was named Joe Bertony. This monument was created because it was meant to help mould a better and more enlightened community. More than 10.9 million people visit the Opera house yearly.
